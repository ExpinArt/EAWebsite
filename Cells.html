Cells

In biology, cells are defined as the basic unit of life. All living things are made from one or more cells. A fundamental concept. A building block. A small unit that groups together to form tissues, from tissues to organs, organs to organisms, organisms to communities, communities to civilizations. Hoechst is a chemical that can be used to stain cells by binding to DNA. Imagine a chemical that allows us to see the very unit that builds us and everything we know of the living world. A peak into ourselves, a peak into the world. Cells.
