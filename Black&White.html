Black and White Series

The concept is simple. We want to showcase our experiments in creative ways. Working on the central nervous system, we take hundreds of photos of the brain using high-resolution fluorescence microscopes. One drawback of research is that majority of its data just gets stored or placed in books and shelves that a lot of people don’t have access to. This leaves us with very little appreciation for science, especially if you are not in the industry. This is where we want Experiments in Art to come to place. The goal is to have conversations about science and art between people coming from different industries. After all, science and art is about storytelling and Experiments in Art is a tool for these stories to be told.
