Pink Brain

Scientists read a bunch of papers. It's part of the job description. Whenever we're not doing experiments, we read, study, and write. We read because we believe it'll help us understand what we're doing or what we should do next and in many cases that can happen, but similarly, all these information can lead to more confusion and misdirection. So I draw, I paint, I doodle, I try to create.
To re-wire my brain.
To get a breath of fresh air.
To break free from drowning in all the information available
To restart.
